The Market Watch display allows you to simultaneously monitor market data for different security types, such as equities, indices, treasuries, and futures.

To launch the Market Watch display:

  • Enter @MW in <%link(16233,"FactSet Search")%>.

With the Market Watch display, you can:

    • <%link("15884#manage","Enter an identifier")%> in the Identifier box and then press ENTER. Press the DELETE key to remove an identifier.
    • Right-click and select <%link(15886,"Options > Groupings tab")%> to dynamically organize the securities in your portfolio into easy-to-read, collapsible sections.

      Tip To quickly group the companies using <%link("15884#manual_group","manual groupings")%>, select a row and enter G:group_name and then press ENTER.
    • Right-click and select <%link(15887,"Options > Columns tab")%> to customize the columns in the display.



    • Click on the <%link("15892#count", "News Count (#)")%> to display the number of news stories for each source. Highlight and select a news source to launch a <%link(15863,"News")%> display with the corresponding identifier and source.
    • Add the Tick Trend column to track stock movements in real-time. For a more detailed view, double-click the Tick Trend column to launch the <%link(15931,"Chart")%> display.
    • Add the Notes column to include comments in your Market Watch display. To save your comments, you must save the identifiers in the display as a <%link("15884#ticker_lists","ticker list")%> (right-click and select Save Ticker List).
    • To review definitions for columns in your Market Watch display, see <%link(15888,"Column Descriptions and Calculations")%>.
  • Right-click and select Options > Display Settings tab to <%link("15893#gridlines","add gridlines")%> and <%link("15893##currency","change the display currency")%>.

    Tip You can also change the display currency by clicking on the currency name in the Market Watch toolbar. By default, all data in Market Watch is local currency.

    <%Image("e2e8161b-bfb1-463e-8ec3-732abbb8c40b")%>
  • Click the Recalc/Re-sort button <%Image("87aee335-8237-43b0-8ba3-6a064287d838")%> to quickly recalculate statistics and re-sort columns based on the latest data.
  • Right-click and select Format to <%link(15856,"customize the appearance")%> (e.g., colors, fonts, etc) of the data.
  • Right-click in the display and select <%link("15855#pref","Preferences")%> > Save to save the look of the display, including any columns you have added/removed from the display, font information, colors, etc. You can save the preference as your default

The following keyboard shortcuts are also available in Market Watch:

  • To delete an identifier, select the row and press the DELETE key.
  • To insert a row, press the INSERT key.
  • To deselect a row, press ESC.
